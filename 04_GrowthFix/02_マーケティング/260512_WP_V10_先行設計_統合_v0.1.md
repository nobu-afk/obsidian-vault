   - Jenkins 1998 / Cerasoli 2014 / Edmondson 1999 / Detert-Burris 2007 / Kellogg-Valentine 2020 / Tambe-Cappelli 2019 / Takeuchi 2007

   - AI が単一権威の解説は得意・複数領域の編集統合は AI 苦手
